# Returns and RMAs

## Eligibility window

Most non-sterile, non-special-order items can be returned within **30 days**
of delivery for a full refund minus a 15% restocking fee. The clock starts
on the delivery date, not the order date.

## Items that cannot be returned

- Sterile single-use items whose packaging has been opened.
- Refrigerated or temperature-sensitive items (cold-chain integrity cannot
  be re-verified).
- Custom-printed or special-order items (e.g., facility-branded items).
- Items past their expiry date or with less than 6 months of shelf life
  remaining.
- Hazmat / DG-shipped items where the original outer packaging is missing.

## How to start a return (RMA)

1. Email `returns@healthsupplyco.example` with your account number, the
   original sales-order number, the SKU(s), and quantity.
2. We respond within 1 business day with an RMA number and a return label.
3. Pack the items in their original packaging with the RMA number visible
   on the outside of the carton.
4. Ship within 14 days of receiving the RMA — RMAs expire after 14 days.

## Refund timing

Refunds post to the original payment method within 5-10 business days of
receipt and inspection. Net-30 accounts see the credit on the next monthly
statement.

## Damaged or incorrect shipments

If a shipment arrives damaged or with the wrong items, contact your account
manager within **48 hours of delivery**. We replace damaged/incorrect items
at no charge and arrange pickup of the originals — the 15% restocking fee
does not apply.
