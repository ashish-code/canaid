# Ordering and Account Setup

HealthSupplyCo is a B2B distributor. We do not sell directly to consumers.
A facility account is required to place orders.

## Becoming a customer

To open an account:

1. Submit a **New Account Application** via the customer portal or your
   sales contact. We need:
   - Facility name, address, and HST/GST registration (Canada) or
     EIN/state tax ID (US).
   - A medical-license or facility-license number for regulated product
     categories (e.g., chlorhexidine, prescription-adjacent items).
   - Authorized signatory and a primary buyer contact.
2. Credit-check (1-3 business days). Approved accounts receive Net-30 terms;
   unapproved orders ship prepaid.
3. Account activation — typically same-day after credit clears.

## Channels

- **Customer portal** (web, with order history, reordering, status tracking).
- **EDI** (X12 850/810/856) for hospital systems and group purchasing
  organizations. Implementation typically 4-6 weeks.
- **Email or phone** orders accepted from approved accounts.

## Minimum order quantity

Most line items have **MOQ of 1**. A small subset of high-handling-cost items
(elastic bandages, specimen cups) carry per-line MOQs noted on the product
sheet. There is no minimum order *value* for accounts on Net-30 terms.

## Backorders

We rarely backorder, but if a SKU is unavailable when we receive your order:

- We notify within 1 business day with the expected availability date.
- You can choose: hold the order, ship partial now and the balance later
  (no second freight charge), or cancel the line.

## Contract pricing

Group purchasing organization (GPO) members and contracted hospitals receive
contracted pricing automatically — no need to apply codes at checkout.
Pricing inquiries go to your sales contact, not the contact-center
assistant.
