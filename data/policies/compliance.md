# Regulatory Compliance and Quality

HealthSupplyCo distributes only products that meet the regulatory framework
applicable to the destination market.

## Canada

- All Class II / III / IV medical devices carry a valid **Medical Device
  Licence (MDL)** issued by Health Canada. We verify MDL status at
  product-onboarding and re-verify annually.
- Drug products and antiseptics carry a valid **Drug Identification Number
  (DIN)** or **Natural Product Number (NPN)** as applicable.
- Our distribution operations are licensed under a **Medical Device
  Establishment Licence (MDEL)** — license number available on request.

## United States

- Devices distributed into the US carry **FDA 510(k) clearance** or are
  exempt under 21 CFR 880. Clearance numbers appear on the product sheet
  (`certifications` field) where applicable.
- We are registered with FDA as a medical-device distributor.

## Quality management

- Our quality system is certified to **ISO 13485:2016**.
- Lot-traceability is maintained for every shipment. On request, we can
  provide a Certificate of Analysis (COA) or lot-trace report within
  1 business day.
- Recalls are communicated to all affected customers within 24 hours of
  issuance by the manufacturer or competent authority.

## Cold chain

Cold-chain products ship in validated insulated packaging with temperature
indicators. Each shipment includes a USB temperature logger; the data file
is available on request to the receiving facility's quality team.

## Data handling

We do not share customer order data with third parties except as required
by law or by the customer's GPO/contract. Order records are retained for
7 years per regulatory expectations.
