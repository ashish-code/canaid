# Shipping & Lead Times

HealthSupplyCo ships across Canada and the contiguous United States. We do not
ship outside North America at this time.

## Service zones and lead times

| Zone | Region | Standard transit | Express transit |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | GTA, Montreal, Ottawa, Vancouver, Calgary | 1 business day | Same day (cut-off 11:00 local) |
| 2 | Other Canadian metros (pop. > 250k) | 2 business days | Next business day |
| 3 | All other Canadian destinations | 3-5 business days | 1-2 business days |
| 4 | Northern territories, remote postal codes | 5-10 business days | Not available |
| 5 | Contiguous US (warehouse-direct via Buffalo) | 3-5 business days | 2 business days |

Transit times are added to **product lead times** (the per-SKU `lead_time_days`
field). For example, a 3-day-lead-time product shipping to Zone 2 typically
arrives 5 business days after order confirmation.

## Carriers

- **Standard:** Canada Post and Purolator (Canada), UPS Ground (US).
- **Express:** Purolator Next-Day, UPS Next Day Air.
- **Cold-chain / temperature-sensitive:** Cryopak insulated packaging on
  all temperature-sensitive items; refrigerated trucks for orders >100 kg.

## Free shipping threshold

Standard ground shipping is free on orders over **CAD $750** (or USD $550 for
US destinations). Below threshold, a flat-rate shipping fee applies based on
zone (calculated at checkout).

## Hazmat and dangerous-goods

A small subset of products (certain antiseptics, alcohol-based sanitizers in
volumes over 1 L) ship as Class 3 dangerous goods and incur a CAD $35 hazmat
handling fee per shipment. Air-freight is not available for these items.

## Order cut-off

Same-day shipment requires order confirmation **before 14:00 Eastern Time**
on a business day. Orders placed after cut-off ship the next business day.
